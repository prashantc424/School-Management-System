Balaji Computer Centre has the mission of educating the person in the rural part of eastern U.P. In this area of information Technology and Science We are committed to made available the computer education literacy and accounts education on very nominal fee structures covering rural and urban area. Through latest educatiin, we are educating the students to make them skilled regarding industry requirement. With this great mission, Balaji Computer Centre is running it's education centre in MAU Distt.By offering latest courses, we are training people and help them to either get a job in corporate companies or become able to stablish IT based small business and start earning for their life. More then 2000 successful students are live evidance of our commitment and quality education. 'Education for Life' is the key of our education. We better understand the value of your time and money.
